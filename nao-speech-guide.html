1 System Boot

When main.py starts and the wake listener initializes.

Startup Announcement warm
Time-adaptive greeting: Good morning! System is ready. Say 'Nao' to activate me. Good afternoon! System is ready. Say 'Nao' to activate me. Good evening! System is ready. Say 'Nao' to activate me.

2 Wake Activation

User says "Nao". Response depends on proximity.

Close Proximity (< 0.6 meters)
You Say
"Nao"
NAO Says
Good afternoon! I am Nao. Nice to meet you. Would you like to shake my hand? warm
If user says "Yes" → NAO extends right hand for handshake
NAO Says
I hope you are doing great. How may I assist you today? (followed by the full assist menu with pauses between each option)
If user says "No" or timeout → skips handshake
NAO Says
How may I assist you today? Say 'let's chat' for conversation mode. 'Morgan assist' for Morgan State University help. For smart tools, say 'mini nao'. If support is needed, say 'therapist mode'. Ask me to 'dance' or to 'follow you' anytime.
Far Distance (> 0.6 meters)
NAO Says
NAO waves, then speaks: Good afternoon! I am Nao. It is very nice to meet you. How may I assist you today? ... warm

12 Voice Style Reference

NAO adjusts speech speed and pitch using naoqi TTS tags to match the emotional context.

StyleSpeedPitchSounds LikeUsed For
warm 90% 95% Slightly slower, slightly deeper Greetings, farewells, GPT replies, general conversation
excited 110% 110% Faster, higher pitched, energetic Dance intro/outro, milestone celebrations
calm 80% 90% Noticeably slower and deeper Therapist opening, mood prompts, settling moments
thinking 85% 95% Deliberate, slightly lower Error messages, uncertain responses
empathetic 85% 90% Slow and soft Mood responses, therapy replies, emotional validation
neutral 100% 100% Default NAO voice Utility outputs, posture confirmations
All phrases marked with 🎲 are randomized — NAO selects a different variant each time so conversations never feel repetitive. There are 120+ unique phrases across 25 categories.
